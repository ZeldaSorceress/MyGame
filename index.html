Game Details

Background/Story
You are a champion chosen by the Gods and
sworn to protect the people from the incoming threat.
You grew up not having any combat experience, yet you were
destined to be the greatest champion. You must travel to every part
of the world to complete your ultimate quest.
The sealed evil is bound to break free at any given time,
are you the champion that can defeat this evil?
